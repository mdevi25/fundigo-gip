    Team data: org-owned Shared Drive
    
        
    
          Grant data lives in a Google Sheet inside your org's Shared Drive,
          not on any third-party server, and not owned by any one person's account. If a
          volunteer who's a team member leaves, removing them from the
          Shared Drive's membership is all that's needed; the data stays.
        
    
        
    

        
    

        
    
          
    
            
            
          
    
          
          
        
    

        
    
          One-time setup (founder/admin does this once):
          1) In Google Drive, click New → Shared Drive, name it (e.g.
          "Fundigo Grant Data"), and add your grant team as members.
          2) Open it. The URL looks like
          drive.google.com/drive/folders/<ID>
          , copy the <ID> part.
          3) Paste it above and click Save. The team spreadsheet is created
          inside it automatically the first time anyone loads data.
